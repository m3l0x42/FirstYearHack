Education: Health Exercises

Exercise 1: The 20-20-20 Rule

This is the one from the popup! Every 20 minutes, take a break and look at something 20 feet away for at least 20 seconds. This helps reduce digital eye strain.

Exercise 2: Desk Stretches

Perform simple stretches at your desk to prevent muscle stiffness.

  • Neck Tilts: Gently tilt your head from side to side.
  • Shoulder Rolls: Roll your shoulders backwards and forwards.
  • Torso Twists: Gently twist your upper body from side to side.

Exercise 3: Mindful Breathing

Take 60 seconds to focus on your breath. Inhale slowly through your nose, hold for a moment, and exhale slowly through your mouth. This is a great way to reduce stress and reset your focus.
